Abstract

L'ontology TABULAE is an extension of the PeGazUs vocabulary dedicated to the representation of information contained in the French 19th-century land registry documents. It enables the description of land plots, their taxpayers, their nature (with over 150 controlled values), and the changes and events affecting these plots over time. Their evolution is described at the attribute level. The ontology also models the sources involved (mutation registers, section registers, cadastral index maps) and provides support for automatic classification and named entity recognition in digitised documents (anclass, NamedEntity). It builds on the PeGazUs, RiC-O, SKOS and PROV-O ontologies.
